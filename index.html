Projects

Nerine

Nerine is a 3D game developed in Unity using C# in collaboration with Floralia Team

It is an experimental first-person graphic adventure that combines surreal exploration and garden mechanics.

With an aesthetic defined by weirdcore and glitchcore, the game follows Nerine, a girl trying to escape from people and reality, in a world that mixes nature and digital surrealism.

Metro: Metro Santiago de Chile

Metro Metro is an Android game with over 1,000 downloads on the Play Store, based on the Santiago de Chile Metro system. It’s designed to help users learn and memorize the metro network in an interactive way by playing through stations and testing their memory..
